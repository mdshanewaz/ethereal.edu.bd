WHY ETHEREAL?

The modern world is running on the wheels of education. In order to survive in the competitive world, we all need education as a torch that leads the way.

I believe that a good school is a holy place where the light of wisdom is focused and that the teacher is the mysterious laboratory's magical master. They provide the power of education to today‚Äôs youth, thereby giving them the possibility for a better future.

ETHEREAL is the first English Medium School in Feni following Cambridge Curriculum, UK with Islamic education. We have started our journey with a mission and vision to flourish wise and competent leadership who will lead the world in various sectors and fulfill the responsibilities of their society through academic excellence, intellectual growth and moral values.

We are committed to provide state-of-the-art educational facilities, qualified teachers team and a conducive learning environment for learners.

I welcome you all to visit our campus and for the betterment of ETHEREAL your valuable advice is expected.

Wing Commander (Retd) Shah Imran Chowdhury

President, Ethereal English Medium School

Assalamu Alaikum Warahmatullah.

It is my honor and privilege to extend a warm welcome to you on behalf of ETHEREAL, Feni's pioneering English Medium School. As the Head of the School, I am excited to embark on a journey of knowledge, growth, and excellence alongside each of you.

At ETHEREAL, we believe in the transformative power of education. Our commitment to the Cambridge Curriculum, coupled with Islamic education, reflects our dedication to providing a holistic and enriching learning experience. Our aim is not only to impart knowledge but to foster critical thinking, creativity, and moral values in our students.

We take pride in our qualified teaching team, state-of-the-art facilities, and a learning environment designed to inspire and empower. ETHEREAL is more than an institution; it is a community where each student is encouraged to explore their potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Thank you for choosing ETHEREAL. Let us embark on this educational journey hand in hand.

M. Ashraful Hoque Bhuiyan

Head of the School, ETHEREAL.
